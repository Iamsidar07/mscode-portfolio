# Address in ethereum
There are two types of addresses in ethereum -
- EOA (Externally Owned Account)
- Smart Contract Account

## EOA (Externally Owned Account)
**EOA** are controlled by human and needed a private key to sign the transaction. Examples are **Metamask Wallet** etc.
